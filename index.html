Apollo Hospitals Jubilee Hills

Apollo Hospitals Jubilee Hills in Hyderabad was established in 1988 by Dr. Prathap C. Reddy, and it was the first hospital in India to be recognized as a "Health City". Driven by a vision of providing exceptional healthcare, it has become a prominent and trusted integrated healthcare facility in Asia.

Apollo Hospitals Secunderabad

Apollo Hospitals in Secunderabad was established on April 2nd, 2010, as the 50th hospital in the Apollo Hospitals Group. This 150-bed tertiary care facility aims to provide exceptional healthcare, focusing on excellence, proficiency, compassion, and pioneering advancements.

Apollo Hospitals Hyderguda

Apollo Hospitals in Hyderguda, Hyderabad was founded by Prathap C. Reddy, the visionary leader of the Apollo Hospitals Group, in 1983 as the first corporate healthcare facility in India. Prathap C. Reddy is credited with revolutionizing private healthcare in India, establishing a foundation for modern healthcare practices. Apollo Hospitals Hyderguda is now a leading multi-specialty hospital providing comprehensive care across various specialties.

Apollo Hospitals DRDO

Apollo Hospitals DRDO in Hyderabad was formed as a collaborative project between Apollo Hospitals and the Defense Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) to provide healthcare services to Defense Laboratories employees. The hospital officially began operations in 2001, after being established in 1996. It has since expanded to serve the general public in the south zone of Hyderabad and the neighboring Ranga Reddy district.
